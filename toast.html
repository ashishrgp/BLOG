Toast of Affection

Problems look mighty small from 150 miles up

Posted by Ashish Raghupatruni on March 13, 2015

Love is the best phase of your life. It gives a sense of satisfaction and a certain eternal bliss. There are times in our lives, when we tend to strongly infatuate with that one person we surely want to know more about. The best way to be sure about him/her is to ask ourselves whether we feel it or not. We make our lives special in one way or the other but we forget to love. That’s where we start to vitiate the feelings of our heart. Love your parents, Love your partners, Love your friends, and Love everyone. It’s one social cause where most of our society isn’t skilful in. A very important fact though is the adversities contained with being in Love. None of which can’t be restored. An important aspect comes to attention when we get affected with feelings is the fear of losing it again. The point of opinion is to forget about the pessimisms and continue living it. The sweets of a lifetime have been packaged by nature keeping in mind that it fulfils the wishes of its gears. It’s a time of your life when you know that it possesses the ability of providing you with timeless pleasure and unending inclination towards attachment. Love is unconditional and countless. You can’t measure it but you can surely control it. The intimacy, it adds is closely linked to the fact that it provides care and tenderness to all. So be in Love, be grateful and be faithful. This is the time to fall in love and be loved. Welcome everyone to your heart as there is no other feeling that nature offers for warmth.
